Lost or Delayed Packages

If your package is lost or significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team at temblemybookshop@yahoo.com or call [123546978] to report the issue.
  2. Provide your order number and relevant details so we can assist you more effectively.
  3. We will initiate an investigation with the shipping carrier to locate your package.
  4. If the package cannot be located, we will work with you to provide a suitable solution, which may include reshipping the order or issuing a refund.
